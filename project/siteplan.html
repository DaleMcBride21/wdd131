McBride Technologies

Site Name: McBride Technologies

This name was selected because it represents a modern and innovative technology e-commerce store offering the latest tech products and gadgets, reflecting both the family heritage (McBride) and a focus on technology.

Site Purpose:

The site aims to serve as a comprehensive online store for technology enthusiasts, offering a wide range of tech products such as computers, computer parts, and other types of electronics. It will provide detailed product information, user reviews, and a customer support portal.

Scenarios

Scenarios are questions that are asked by a site visitor who represents the target audience of the organization or company. The questions will help you drive the content of the website as it provides answers to the questions. Again, much of the content specifications will actually be provided but start thinking about the target audience of the site. Include at least two scenarios.

Scenarios:

  • What are the best components to build a high-performance gaming PC?
  • Which laptop is best for graphic design?
  • Do you offer international shipping?
  • Are there any discounts available for bulk purchases?
  • How do I contact customer support for technical assistance?
  • What are the warranty options for your products?

Selected Colors: (The text is a little hard to see, but it will be fixed on the actual site. It is just so the focus can be on the picked colors.)

  • Primary Color: #195905 (used for headings and links)
  • Secondary Color: #E6D6B8 (used for accents and call-to-action buttons)
  • Background Color: #36454F (used for the page background)
  • Text Color: #EFEFEF (used for body text and paragraphs)

Selected Fonts:

  • Heading Font: Lato, sans-serif (used for all headings and subheadings)
  • Body Font: Montserrat, sans-serif (used for all body text and paragraphs)
